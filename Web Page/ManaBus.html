About Mana Bus

Our History

Mana Bus was established in 2025 with a mission to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation services to everyone. Starting with just 5 buses, we've grown to become one of the region's most trusted bus service providers.

Our Fleet

We maintain a modern fleet of over 100 buses, including AC sleeper coaches, semi-sleeper buses, and express non-AC buses. All our vehicles undergo regular maintenance and safety checks to ensure your journey is comfortable and secure.

Our Team

Our team of professional drivers and customer service staff are trained to prioritize your safety and comfort. With an average of 10 years of experience, our drivers know the routes like the back of their hand.

Our Values

- Safety first: Regular maintenance and safety checks

- Customer comfort: Spacious seating and onboard amenities

- Punctuality: We pride ourselves on timely departures and arrivals

- Affordable pricing: Quality service at reasonable rates
